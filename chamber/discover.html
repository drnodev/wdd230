Discover El Salvador

El Salvador, officially the Republic of El Salvador, is a sovereign country in Central America located on the coast of the Pacific Ocean with a land area of 21,041 km. Tourists visiting El Salvador come mainly from the United States and Canada, Central American countries and European countries, El Salvador is the second Central American country that received the most tourists in 2019, while in 2021, it was the Central American country that received the most tourists, with a total of 1.4 million tourists.

El Tunco (a very Salvadoran word for pigs), is the most emblematic surfing destination in El Salvador.
Located just 7 kilometers west of the port of La Libertad, El Tunco is a microcosm where you can breathe a cosmopolitan atmosphere, abundant fun and "chiva" wave, as they say locally to express something great.

This is a very special celebration for the inhabitants of Nejapa and also for many people from other departments and foreigners who visit it, because all the streets of Nejapa are filled with this very peculiar atmosphere, although at the same time it is very dangerous.
